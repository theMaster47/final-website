Hobbies

Spending time with family
Nothing is more important to me than family. I would do anything for them. I enjoy spending time with them, whether it be game night, movie night, or just a walk in the park.
Singing and dancing
I was born and raised in the midwest so show choir was a big part of my life. Both my older sisters were in it and I loved it with every fiber of my being. I did it for over five years and was a captain for close to three of those years. I still love singing with my family or on my own and enjoy going swing dancing.
Frisbee golf and ultimate frisbee
Ever since I was young, I loved frisbee, whether it be my dad and I trying to see if we can hit that tree, or playing it in gym. In high school, I excelled at it. And playing frisbee golf with my dad will always bring me great memories of my childhood.
